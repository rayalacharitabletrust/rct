Spiritual Significance

A Kruthayuga Kshetra

According to the Brahmanda Purana, Ahobilam is the Avatara Sthala of Bhagavan Narasimha. It is one of the **108 Sri Vaishnava Divya Desams**.

The great Sri Thirumangai Alwar, one of the 12 Alwars who lived in the 8th century AD, visited this holy place and composed ten *Pasurams* (hymns) dedicating them to the Lord of Ahobilam.

Divya Desam
One of the holiest 108 Vishnu temples.

Important Policies

Terms & Conditions

By booking a stay, you agree to abide by the following rules.

  • ID Proof & Eligibility

    All guests must present a valid Government ID proof (Aadhaar, Driving License, Voter ID, etc.) at the time of check-in. Guests under the age of 18 are not allowed without guardians. Unmarried couples and single guests are strictly not allowed.

  • Strict Prohibitions

    Consumption of Alcohol, Smoking, and Non-Vegetarian food is strictly prohibited inside the premises. Illegal items are not allowed.

  • Check-in/Out & Late Arrival

    Standard Check-in/Check-out time is 24 Hours. Guests must reach before 3:00 PM if opting for Pay at Check-in. Late arrivals must inform in advance, otherwise the booking may be cancelled. Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability and may be chargeable.

    Bookings once confirmed are non-refundable. In case of gate closing rules or failure to check-in, no refund will be provided. The management is not responsible for cancellations due to unavoidable situations.

    Room capacity limits are strictly followed. Amenities like AC, Hot Water, etc., are subject to availability and power supply. Management reserves the right to change facilities without prior notice.

    The management is not responsible for loss or theft of personal items. Guests are responsible for their belongings. Admission is reserved for Yatris following religious principles.
