The 9 Holy Abodes

Ahobilam is the only place where all nine forms of Lord Narasimha are worshipped.
(అహోబిలంలోని నవ నారసింహ క్షేత్రాలు మరియు దర్శన వేళలు)

Nava Narasimhas

  • 1. Jwala Narasimha: The anger form (Upper Ahobilam)
  • 2. Ahobila Narasimha: The main deity (Upper Ahobilam)
  • 3. Malola Narasimha: With Goddess Lakshmi (Peaceful)
  • 4. Kroda Narasimha: Varaha Form
  • 5. Karanja Narasimha: Under Karanja Tree
  • 6. Bhargava Narasimha: Worshipped by Parashurama
  • 7. Yogananda Narasimha: In Yoga posture
  • 8. Chatravata Narasimha: Under Peepal tree
  • 9. Pavana Narasimha: The most powerful form

How to Reach Ahobilam

Ahobilam is well connected by Road and Rail networks.
(అహోబిలం చేరుకొను మార్గాలు)

By Road (బస్సు ద్వారా)

Ahobilam is 25 KM from Allagadda and 65 KM from Nandyal. Direct APSRTC buses are available from Allagadda Bus Stand every 30 minutes. Pilgrims coming from Hyderabad/Bangalore can get down at Allagadda.

By Train (రైలు ద్వారా)

The nearest Railway Stations are:

  • Nandyal (NDL): 65 KM away.
  • Cuddapah (HX): 85 KM away.
  • Tadipatri (TU): 100 KM away.
From there, you can take a bus or taxi to Allagadda/Ahobilam.

By Air (విమానం ద్వారా)

The nearest airports are:

  • Kurnool (Orvakal): 110 KM.
  • Tirupati (TIR): 230 KM.
  • Bengaluru (BLR): 350 KM.
  • Hyderabad (RGIA): 330 KM.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries regarding accommodation, booking, and facilities.
(భక్తులకు సాధారణంగా వచ్చే సందేహాలు - సమాధానాలు)

You can book a room by clicking on the "Book Room" button on our website and filling out the enquiry form. Alternatively, you can directly call our office numbers provided in the contact section. We recommend booking at least 1 week in advance during festival seasons and weekends to ensure availability. Please carry a valid Govt ID Proof (Aadhaar Card) during check-in.

Rayala Charitable Trust is a non-profit organization. While the accommodation is provided as a service to devotees, we collect **Nominal Maintenance Charges** to cover electricity, cleaning, water, and staff salaries. These charges are very affordable compared to commercial hotels.

(మాది సేవా దృక్పథంతో నడిచే ట్రస్ట్. కరెంట్ మరియు నిర్వహణ ఖర్చుల కోసం కేవలం నామమాత్రపు రుసుము మాత్రమే తీసుకోబడుతుంది).

As Ahobilam is a sacred pilgrimage site, we strictly follow spiritual discipline:
  • Prohibited: Consumption of Alcohol, Smoking, and Non-Vegetarian food is STRICTLY PROHIBITED inside the premises.
  • Silence: Guests are requested to maintain silence and keep the premises clean.
  • Unmarried Couples: Rooms will not be allotted to unmarried couples. Proper family ID proofs are mandatory.

Hot Water: Yes, we have solar heating systems and geysers ensuring 24/7 hot water supply, which is essential for devotees taking an early morning bath before Darshan.
Food: We provide Nitya Annadanam (Free Food) to all inmates staying in our rooms. There are also several canteens nearby for tiffin and snacks.

Our standard check-in time is 24 Hours from the time of arrival (subject to availability). However, during peak festival days, fixed timings might apply. We request you to confirm your arrival time over the phone so we can keep the room ready for you.

Yes, we have spacious Car Parking facility within the trust compound, which is safe and secure. The main Lower Ahobilam temple is at a walkable distance, and auto-rickshaws are readily available to travel to Upper Ahobilam.
