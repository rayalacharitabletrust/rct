Our Main Seva

"Serving humanity is serving the divinity."
Join us in our mission to feed the devotees of Lord Narasimha.

Nitya Annadanam

"Annadhanam is the most sacred of all charities."

At Rayala Charitable Trust, we believe that no devotee visiting the holy abode of Ahobilam should go hungry. We provide free, hygienic, and wholesome meals to pilgrims every day. Your small contribution helps us serve hot meals to hundreds of devotees who trek the difficult hills of Nallamalla to see the Lord.

Our Future Vision

With the blessings of Lord Narasimha, we plan to start Go Seva (Cow Protection) and Vidya Daanam (Education Support) very soon.
